Meet the Founders

Alice Crisci is a cancer survivor, entrepreneur and activist. She founded Ovum Medical as a science and technology company to launch the global digital health application MedAnswer to help people in health crisis connect to the specialist they need quickly. She also founded the oncofertility charity, Fertile Action, three weeks into her breast cancer diagnosis at 31-years-old.
